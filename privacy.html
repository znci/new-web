Privacy Policy for znci

At znci, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information.

We collect information about you when you use our services, including:

and any other information you choose to provide. We also collect information about your device, such as:

Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How do we protect your information?

We take the security of your personal information seriously and use appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or receive from us.
